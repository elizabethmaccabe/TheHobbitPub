ABOUT US

The Hobbit Pub, located close to Southampton University on Bevois Valley Road, is a late night entertainment venue and Traditional English Pub rolled into one. With its split levels, two bars, huge beer garden, Bag End Burgers and the very best live music entertainment; there is something for all. Popular with students, and like-minded locals, the atmosphere simply oozes from the bare brick walls every night as the pub fills to capacity.

We host international, national and local bands and have music festivals running throughout the year including our Ale festival, Cider festival and Messtival, for those of you who like your music a little rougher around the edges. Or come down and chill out to out Open Mic Night and if you have talent, bring it! Being so close to the University at Highfield, the student population of Southampton seems to find our pub a welcoming home to take their minds off their studies for a while and to socialise at.
