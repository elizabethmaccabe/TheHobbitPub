T-SHIRT QUESTS

Complete our quests to receive limited edition Hobbit t-shirts.

  • The Fellowship Quest

    Ask for a Fellowship Quest at the bar and bring it along on every visit to gain a stamp for each of The Fellowship cocktails that you purchase.

    Upon completion of The Fellowship, you will receive a limited edition Hobbit t-shirt and become eligible to purchase the Nazgûl cocktail and enter The Nine Quest.

    It is not wise to complete The Fellowship Quest in one night, which is why this quest has no time restriction. The Hobbit Pub encourages you to drink moderately and responsibly.

  • The Nine Quest

    Complete The Fellowship Quest and you qualify to enter the dark world of The Nine. Ask for a Nine Quest at the bar and bring it along on every visit to gain a stamp for each of The Nine cocktails that you purchase.

    It is not wise to complete The Fellowship Quest in one night, which is why this quest has no time restriction. The Hobbit Pub encourages you to drink moderately and responsibly.
